    Gray Marketing Services

    Digital Advertising

    Digital Advertising generates roughly 400 billion dollars in revenue each year, and is continually growing. It’s no secret that successful companies spend money in this space because they know the hold the internet has over their customers. GRAY Marketing Solutions was founded on our abilities to grow businesses through Pay Per Click (PPC) Advertising. We start off our process by learning about our clients business, reviewing their current marketing strategy, and performing a competitor audit. Our approach to these techniques allow our custom plan to be specific for each client to increase their ROI. After these steps are taken, we will decide which PPC platform (Facebook, Google, Amazon, etc.) will be best paired with your business model. Once the campaigns are launched, we will continually monitor the analytics and modify the ads to create consistent conversions.

    Web Design

    In today's growing digital space, a company's website is often the portal for potential customers to enter your universe. Most consumers decide whether they like your website or not within the first 10 seconds, which makes an engaging and memorable design key to success. Here at GRAY Marketing Solutions we understand the customer experience matters, which is why our team constantly looks for ways to make your company stand out over the competition. We begin building your website by understanding what makes our customers brand unique and performing a competitor analysis. Once our team has some ideas to run with they can start construction of a website that is filled with impactful storytelling, modern aesthetics, simple navigation, and stunning visuals. Each of our websites are responsive and mobile friendly while able to seamlessly implement third party applications. Websites are no longer just a tool, they are a piece of digital real estate that collects large amounts of data on user behaviors and interactions with your company. It's GRAY Marketing Solutions’s job to give you a way to unlock that information to generate sales.

    Logo Design

    At GRAY Marketing Solutions, we create the most memorable and timeless graphic design pieces that align with our customers' branding. We understand that your logo needs to convey the vision and innovation of your work to others. GRAY Marketing Solutions takes pride in offering our clients a trouble-free process that gives them expert designs with intricacies beyond belief. The first step would be to discuss your brand’s image and allow us to communicate your core values. Once the immersion into “who you are” is complete we can then produce 3 logos that convey the brand to the public. Once completed, clients will have unlimited revisions until they approve of the final product. GRAY Marketing Solutions will also include all source files of the final logo design.

    SEO Optimization

    Search Engine Optimization or SEO’s are a key puzzle piece for websites and businesses that want to rank high on major search engines. GRAY Marketing Solutions has recognized their role in generating the proper traffic to convert customers vs. an influx of uninterested traffic. This process starts with an audit of our clients current marketing strategies and a deep dive into keyword research. In our research phase, we will identify keywords that would give the best opportunities for targeting and use them to create a personalized proposal. GRAY Marketing Solutions can offer many routes to advance rankings on search engines through refining clients websites, building backlinks, and content creation. Although these services are ongoing, we don’t stop there! GRAY Marketing Solutions produces a detailed analysis of our work for the client.

    Email Marketing

    Although email isn’t as awe-inspiring as it once was, it is still an extremely useful tool in the digital advertising space. At GRAY Marketing Solutions we will use an email blast marketing program to build a reach beyond just advertisements. We will assist our clients in making a series of eye catching and informative emails to be used on a target audience. As this process builds over time, we will create an email library with an audience interested in your company's message. Additionally, we can create fully automated emails to provide immediate responses and actions to help in the flow of daily business. Some examples include, but not limited to, email responses, sign up forms, and informational blasts.
