    PPC Advertising

    Digital Advertising generates roughly 400 billion dollars in revenue each year, and is continually growing. It’s no secret that successful companies spend money in this space because they know the hold the internet has over their customers. GRAY Marketing Solutions was founded on our abilities to grow businesses through Pay Per Click (PPC) Advertising.

    We start off our process by learning about our clients business, reviewing their current marketing strategy, and performing a competitor audit. Our approach to these techniques allow our custom plan to be specific for each client to increase their ROI. After these steps are taken, we will decide which PPC platform (Facebook, Google, Amazon, etc.) will be best paired with your business model. Once the campaigns are launched, we will continually monitor the analytics and modify the ads to create consistent conversions.

    Website Design

    In today's growing digital space, a company's website is often the portal for potential customers to enter your universe. Most consumers decide whether they like your website or not within the first 10 seconds, which makes an engaging and memorable design key to success. Here at GRAY Marketing Solutions we understand the customer experience matters, which is why our team constantly looks for ways to make your company stand out over the competition. We begin building your website by understanding what makes our customers brand unique and performing a competitor analysis.

    Once our team has some ideas to run with they can start construction of a website that is filled with impactful storytelling, modern aesthetics, simple navigation, and stunning visuals. Each of our websites are responsive and mobile friendly while able to seamlessly implement third party applications. Websites are no longer just a tool, they are a piece of digital real estate that collects large amounts of data on user behaviors and interactions with your company. It's GRAY Marketing Solutions’s job to give you a way to unlock that information to generate sales.
